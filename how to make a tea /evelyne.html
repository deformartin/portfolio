How to make a ginger tea

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On to of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart.

ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Sugar/Honey/vanila sugar (Optional)

preparation

  1. Blend your ginger.
  2. Sieve and separate the chufs.
  3. Add some sugar to according to your taste.
  4. Add drops of lemon to make it more tasty.
  5. Add some Water till you see no hip of giger particles.
  6. Leave the resul overnight to process.
